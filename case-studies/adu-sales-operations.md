# ADU Sales Operations System

## Problem

A design, engineering, and construction company needed a better sales operations system for follow-up, proposal generation, contract generation, and SLA management.

## Solution

Migrated and implemented a Monday.com CRM workflow that improved sales team execution through structured pipeline stages, automated proposal/contract generation, and SLA tracking.

## Known Outcome

Improved sales team close rates by approximately 30%.
