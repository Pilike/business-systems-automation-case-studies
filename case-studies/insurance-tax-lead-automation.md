# Insurance And Tax Lead Automation

## Problem

A lead-driven services business needed to turn Facebook and webhook leads into normalized CRM accounts, opportunities, and customer product records.

## Solution

Built Make workflows that captured leads, used AI to normalize multilingual contact fields, validated phone/email data, mapped CRM picklist values, and created Fireberry records.

## Workflow Capabilities

- Facebook Lead Ads intake.
- Multilingual name, phone, and email parsing.
- CRM account/opportunity creation.
- Product/service record generation.
- Picklist lookup utility flows.
- Missing-data handling and routing.

## Systems

Make, Fireberry CRM, Facebook Lead Ads, OpenAI, webhooks, HTTP modules.

## Portfolio Value

This case demonstrates AI-assisted data normalization inside a CRM implementation, especially for messy lead sources and multilingual inputs.
