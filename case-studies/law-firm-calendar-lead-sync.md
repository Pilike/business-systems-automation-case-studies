# Law Firm Calendar And Lead Sync

## Problem

A legal services workflow needed calendar synchronization between CRM and Outlook, plus website lead intake into CRM.

## Solution

Implemented Make scenarios to sync CRM events and Outlook events in both directions, while also creating CRM records from website leads.

## Workflow Capabilities

- Fireberry-to-Outlook calendar event creation/update.
- Outlook-to-Fireberry event capture.
- Manual repair/test variants for operational support.
- Website lead intake.
- Contact and event mapping.

## Systems

Make, Fireberry CRM, Outlook Calendar, website webhook intake.

## Portfolio Value

This case demonstrates integration design, synchronization risk awareness, and CRM implementation experience in a sensitive professional-services context.
