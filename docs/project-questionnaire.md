# Project Detail Questionnaire

Use these prompts to recover missing project details without exposing confidential data.

## For Each Company/Project

- What business problem existed before the system?
- Who used the system daily?
- What intake channels existed? Forms, email, phone, website, Facebook, CRM webhooks, manual entry?
- What CRM/database objects were involved?
- What were the most important workflow stages/statuses?
- What automations did you personally design or build?
- What had to be configured inside Fireberry, Airtable, Monday.com, or another system outside Make?
- What errors or edge cases did the workflow handle?
- What manual work was reduced?
- Was there a measurable result? Time saved, faster response, fewer duplicates, close-rate improvement, fewer missed tasks?
- What would you improve if rebuilding it today?

## Airtable Estimating And Bookkeeping

- What tables existed? Jobs, customers, estimates, line items, expenses, invoices, payments, vendors, employees?
- Did estimates generate documents or PDFs?
- Did bookkeeping include expenses, receivables, payables, or job profitability?
- Were there role-based views for office, field, management, or accounting?
- Were there Airtable automations, scripts, forms, or interfaces?
- What was the before/after process?

## ADU Sales Operations

- What system did the company use before Monday.com?
- What pipeline stages did you create?
- How were proposals/contracts generated?
- What SLA rules existed?
- What reminders or automations improved sales follow-up?
- How was the 30% close-rate improvement measured?
- Did you integrate email, forms, files, signatures, or accounting?

## Make/Fireberry Automations

- Which flows were production-critical?
- Which were one-time migration or repair flows?
- Which flows are legacy and should be described only as "earlier implementation"?
- Were any flows rebuilt later in Noga/n8n or another system?
- Which flows had the best business impact?
